# 🎮 Game Tier Ranking System (Personal Experience-Based)

## 📌 About This Repository

This repository contains my **personal tier rankings of video games**, based entirely on my own gameplay experience, analysis, and expectations as a player.

Unlike generic rankings, this project focuses on:

* Real player perspective
* Gameplay depth and design quality
* Long-term engagement and replayability

> ⚠️ Note: These rankings are subjective and reflect my personal opinions as a gamer and aspiring game developer.

---

## 🧠 Purpose of This Project

* To analyze games critically rather than just play them
* To understand what makes a game **great, average, or disappointing**
* To improve my own **game design thinking**
* To showcase my ability to evaluate systems, mechanics, and player experience

---

## 🏆 Tier System

The following tier system is used across all rankings:

### 🔥 Z Tier — Legendary / Near Perfect

* Genre-defining or industry-changing games
* Extremely polished with minimal flaws
* High replayability and emotional or gameplay impact
* Sets a benchmark for other games

---

### ⭐ S Tier — Exceptional

* Outstanding gameplay and design
* Very few noticeable flaws
* Highly engaging and memorable
* Could become Z tier with minor improvements

---

### 🅰️ A Tier — Great

* Strong overall experience
* Polished and enjoyable
* Some minor issues or lack of innovation
* Still highly recommended

---

### 🅱️ B Tier — Good

* Fun and playable
* Lacks depth, innovation, or long-term engagement
* May feel repetitive over time
* Good but not remarkable

---

### 🟡 C Tier — Average

* Mediocre experience
* Noticeable flaws in gameplay or design
* Limited replayability
* Only enjoyable in short sessions

---

### ⚠️ E Tier — Poor

* Major gameplay or design issues
* Frustrating mechanics or lack of polish
* Not enjoyable for most players

---

### 💀 F Tier — Broken / Unacceptable

* Severely flawed or unfinished
* Game-breaking bugs or terrible design
* Not worth playing

---

## ⚙️ Ranking Criteria

Each game is evaluated based on:

* 🎮 **Gameplay Mechanics** – Responsiveness, depth, and satisfaction
* 🧠 **Game Design** – Systems, balance, and creativity
* 🔁 **Replayability** – Long-term engagement
* 🎨 **Visual & Audio Design** – Aesthetic quality and immersion
* 🤖 **AI & Difficulty** – Fairness and challenge
* 🌐 **Multiplayer (if applicable)** – Stability and experience
* 🚀 **Innovation** – Does it bring something new?
